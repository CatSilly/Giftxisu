## Reporting a Vulnerability

To protect our players and prevent exploits (cheating/data theft), we do not accept security reports via public Issues or social media.

**Please use GitHub's Private Vulnerability Reporting feature:**

1. Navigate to the **Security** tab of this repository.
2. Click on **Advisories** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **Report a vulnerability** to open a private draft.
4. Provide a detailed description, including steps to reproduce the exploit (Proof of Concept).

By using this method, you can collaborate with us privately to fix the issue before it's disclosed to the public.

## What to Report
We are particularly interested in reports related to:
* **Remote Code Execution (RCE)**
* **Cross-Site Scripting (XSS)** * **Database/Player Data Leaks**
* **Game-breaking exploits** (that bypass payment or core logic)

> We appreciate your help in keeping our game fair and secure for everyone!
